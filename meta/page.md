# Nico Chat

Bring the iconic Japanese video sharing site 'NicoNico Douga' style scrolling chat right to your Twitch stream!

### Features
- **Scrolling Chat**: Transforms standard Twitch messages into flying texts that scroll horizontally across the screen.
- **Full Customization**: Control scrolling speeds, text colors, and the maximum number of simultaneous messages from the control panel.
- **Emotes Support**: Natively renders static and animated emotes seamlessly mixed inside your chat texts including 7TV, BTTV, FFZ and native Twitch ones.
- **Bot Exclusions**: Ignore spam or bot messages easily to keep your screen clean using the 'Ignored Users' list.
